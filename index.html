Krolik Saxophone Ensemble

The Krolik Saxophone Band is an undergraduate organization at Yale College, located in New Haven, CT, USA. We are determined to show that the saxophone can play every kind of music. Since the classical saxophone is particularly rarely heard, our repertoire has a slight emphasis on classical music. We are also determined to give concerts that are enjoyable for everyone, even the audience! If you play sax and live in the New Haven area, we'd love to have you! If you're interested in playing with us, would like to book us for a concert, or have any other questions, contact kroliksaxband@gmail.com. A brief audition is required. We rehearse regularly on Sundays at 4:00-5:30pm.

You can listen to us at Youtube. We're also on Facebook and Ublend.
